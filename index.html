AsteroidsGame
W to accelerate, S to deccelerate, D to turn right, A to turn left, or use Arrow Keys, H to hyperspace, R to add more Asteroids, G to hyperspace into asteroids, SpaceBar to rapidfire singular bullets, E to rapidfire 10 stacked bullets, C to Center the Ship, P to remove all objects except the ship, Q to fire a ring of bullets - Must have <100 bullets onscreen to fire again.
